    This moldable gel is an easy way to achieve 3D art designs. 5 ML.

    The formula allows the nail artist to create their design with no time restraint before curing in the light. Create the same designs as with acrylic, without the hassle of working fast. 

    Recommended to use with alcohol to allow more fluidity when working with this 3D gel, you can use 3D brush, slim shady brush, or silicone tools to carve and sculpt your designs! 

    Cure Time LED/UV: LED: 30-60 sec UV: 60-120 sec *longer cure time for larger pieces, suggested in layers*
    use gloves while working with this product*💖
